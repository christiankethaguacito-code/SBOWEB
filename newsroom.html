Voices in White rally students against corruption

October 1, 2025 By SBO Communications Council

SKSU students gathered in white to affirm integrity, justice, and shared responsibility in the nationwide stand against corruption.

Messages on the solidarity wall captured their hopes for transparent leadership and a principled future for the Filipino youth.

𝗩𝗼𝗶𝗰𝗲𝘀 𝗶𝗻 𝗪𝗵𝗶𝘁𝗲, 𝗦𝘁𝗿𝗲𝗻𝗴𝘁𝗵 𝗶𝗻 𝗧𝗿𝘂𝘁𝗵 🤍

In solidarity with the national call against corruption, SKSU students stood united—wearing white as a symbol of purity, integrity, and hope. Through the whiteboard of aspirations, they spoke truth, wrote their visions, and pledged their commitment to be stewards of justice and reform.

Together, we embody the spirit of the Filipino youth: courageous, principled, and unwavering in the fight for a better nation. 🇵🇭
