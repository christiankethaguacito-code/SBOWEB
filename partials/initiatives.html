How the evaluation flow works

Step 1

Scan or open the form

Organizers display the QR codes at the venue. You can always revisit this hub if you need the link later.

Step 2

Answer honestly

Use your SKSU email. Your notes go straight to the hosting council and the SBO advisers for review.

Step 3

Watch the improvements

Action items are summarised during the next general assembly. Urgent matters are escalated within 24 hours.
