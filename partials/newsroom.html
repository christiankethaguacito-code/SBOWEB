Michael Abad secures Top 2 USG Senator seat

October 18, 2025 By SBO Communications Council

Sultan Kudarat State University - Isulan Campus proudly congratulates Michael Abad for securing the Top 2 spot as University Student Government (USG) Senator.

His victory reflects dedication, passion, and an unwavering commitment to student leadership. As one of the voices chosen to represent the student body, his success is a triumph for every student who believes in service with purpose and leadership with integrity.

Congratulations, Michael Abad - Top 2 USG Senator! Continue to serve with excellence, lead with compassion, and inspire with action.
