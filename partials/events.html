Planning Milestones

Window 1 — Consultations

September College councils submit preferred dates and venue requests for assemblies and forums.
October Coordination meetings with campus units to lock logistics, hosts, and safety protocols.
Ongoing Adviser validation and documentation prep for publication.

Window 2 — Execution

Confirmed date Event brief, run-of-show, and volunteer roster shared with participating orgs.
Event day On-site coordination with marshals, documentation teams, and partner agencies.
Immediately after Quick debrief to capture issues, wins, and data for reporting.

Window 3 — Reporting

Within 7 days Submit attendance sheets, media, and expense notes to the SBO Secretariat.
Within 14 days Publish recap to Newsroom or circulate post-event memo to participants.
Monthly Update this calendar with verified highlights and lessons learned.
